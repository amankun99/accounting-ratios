Complete Accounting Ratios Guide

Liquidity Ratios

1
Current Ratio
Current Assets / Current Liabilities
Measures short-term ability to pay obligations
2
Liquid Ratio (Quick Ratio)
Liquid Asset / Current Liabilities
Assesses immediate liquidity without inventory

Solvency/Leverage Ratios

3
Debt-to-Equity Ratio
Debt / Shareholders’ Equity
Evaluates financial leverage and risk
4
Total Assets to Debt Ratio
Total Assets / Debt
Shows asset coverage for debt obligations
5
Proprietary Ratio
Shareholders’ Equity / Total Assets
Proportion of assets funded by equity
7
Debt to Capital Employed Ratio
Long-Term Debt / Capital Employed
Debt as percentage of total capital
6
Interest Coverage Ratio
Profit before Interest and Tax / Interest on Long-Term Debt
Ability to pay interest obligations

Turnover Ratios

8
Inventory Turnover Ratio
Cost of Revenue from Operations / Average Inventory
Speed of inventory sales and restocking
9
Trade Receivable Turnover Ratio
Credit Revenue from Operations / Average Trade Receivables
Efficiency in collecting receivables
10
Trade Payable Turnover Ratio
Net Credit Purchases / Average Trade Payables
Speed of paying suppliers
11
Working Capital Turnover Ratio
Revenue From operations / Working Capital
Efficiency of working capital usage
12
Fixed Assets Turnover Ratio
Revenue From operations / Net Fixed Assets
Revenue generated from fixed assets
13
Net Assets/Capital Employed Turnover Ratio
Revenue From operations / Capital Employed
Efficiency of capital utilization

Profitability Ratios

14
Gross Profit Ratio
(Gross Profit / Revenue From operations) × 100
Profitability after deducting COGS
15
Operating Ratio
(Cost of Revenue From operations + Operating Expenses) / Revenue From operations × 100
Operational efficiency (lower is better)
16
Operating Profit Ratio
(Operating Profit / Revenue From operations) × 100
Profitability from core operations
17
Net Profit Ratio
(Net Profit After Tax / Revenue From operations) × 100
Overall profitability after taxes
18
ROI/Return on Capital Employed (ROCE)
(Profit before Interest, Tax and Dividend / Capital Employed) × 100
Returns generated on total capital invested
